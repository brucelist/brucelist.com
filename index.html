What makes one agent ask for full commission while another one struggles to list for less?

Beware of Commission Discounts

Struggling agents don't believe in their negotiating and marketing techniques.

They demonstrate this weakness at the beginning by negotiating down their own commission with you.

Inflated Price Evaluations

Some agents are concerned only with generating new listings; they often assume their listing will sell eventually even if it takes several months and price reductions to do so.

Be cautious of any agent who focuses on an inflated price evaluation of your property to win over your business.
